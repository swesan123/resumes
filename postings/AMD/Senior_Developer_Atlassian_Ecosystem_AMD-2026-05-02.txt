Responsibilities
The Role

We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Developer with deep expertise in the Atlassian ecosystem to design, build, and enhance solutions that improve organizational efficiency and support strategic business objectives. This role requires a strong analytical mindset, excellent logical and strategic thinking skills, and the ability to translate complex business needs into scalable technical solutions.

THE PERSON

The ideal candidate is a fast learner and critical thinker with a strong technical foundation in Atlassian application development, API integrations, and AI‑related technologies. You should be comfortable analyzing business processes, gathering requirements, and architecting solutions that deliver measurable impact.

Key Responsibilities

Design and develop custom solutions using python, java scripts, Atlassian Forge, REST APIs, automation frameworks, AI development, Database.
Act as the Subject Matter Expert (SME) for Jira, Confluence, and related Atlassian tools , integration with other applications, providing technical guidance and best practices.
Conduct structured problem analysis, system design, and high‑quality software development following modern engineering practices.

Collaborate with cross‑functional teams to translate business requirements into sound technical designs and implementation plans.
